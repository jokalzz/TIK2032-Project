Astra Yao

Zenless Zone Zero Character Profile

Character Details

Name: Astra Yao
Faction: Victoria Housekeeping Co.
Weapon: Chain Whip
Element: Electric

Build & Strategy

My Astra build focuses on her excellent electric damage and area control abilities. She provides both substantial damage and utility with her wide-range attacks and control effects.

Optimal Rotation

Opening with her chain slash for the electric field effect, then use her special attack to pull enemies together. Follow with her ultimate for massive AoE damage when enemies are grouped up.

Team Synergy

Works well with characters who can further group enemies or extend status effects. Her electric fields synergize with other characters who can trigger chain reactions.

Why I Love This Character

Astra's chain whip moves are visually spectacular, with lightning arcing between enemies and creating dynamic combat situations. Her mobility in battle makes her extremely fun to play, allowing for quick repositioning and crowd control.

The contrast between her polite maid personality and her ferocious fighting style creates an interesting character dynamic. Her backstory as someone who maintains a perfect professional demeanor while harboring incredible combat skills makes her character development particularly interesting.

She's become my go-to character for dealing with multiple enemies, especially in challenging Hollow content where crowd control is essential.
