Alexandrina Sebastine

Zenless Zone Zero Character Profile

Character Details

Name: Alexandrina Sebastine
Faction: Belobog Heavy Industries
Weapon: Dual Pistols
Element: Fire

Build & Strategy

My Alexandrina (Rina) build capitalizes on her exceptional fire damage output and rapid attack sequences. She's a ranged specialist who excels at consistent DPS from a safe distance.

Optimal Rotation

Begin with her charged shot to apply burn status, then use her rapid-fire combo to build meter quickly. Her special attack provides a short damage window, which is perfect for following up with her ultimate for devastating burst damage.

Team Synergy

Works best with characters who can provide shields or draw enemy attention, allowing her to freely deal damage. Characters who can group enemies also help her maximize her AoE potential.

Why I Love This Character

Rina's dual-wielding gunplay is incredibly satisfying, with slick animations and impactful sound effects. Her mobility and range give her gameplay a unique feel compared to many melee-focused characters in the game.

Her character arc as a strict professional business executive with a hidden passion for combat provides a fascinating duality. The contrast between her formal business attire and her aggressive combat style creates a visually distinctive character.

I particularly enjoy how her abilities allow for precision targeting while dealing significant damage. Her ultimate ability, which creates a field of intense fire damage, is not only visually spectacular but strategically valuable for area denial.
