Hoshimi Miyabi

Zenless Zone Zero Character Profile

Character Details

Name: Hoshimi Miyabi
Faction: Cunning Hares
Weapon: Sword
Element: Physical

Build & Strategy

My Miyabi build focuses on maximizing physical damage output while maintaining good survivability. She excels at both single target and AoE damage, making her versatile for various content.

Optimal Rotation

Start with her basic attack combo to build meter, then use her special move to apply the defense debuff. Follow up with her ultimate for maximum damage during the vulnerability window.

Team Synergy

Pairs exceptionally well with support characters who can provide attack buffs or defense shreds. Her fast attacks also make her great at triggering assist effects.

Why I Love This Character

Miyabi combines an elegant aesthetic with a fierce fighting style. Her sword techniques are incredibly satisfying to execute, and her character development throughout the story is compelling. She's been my main DPS since I first obtained her, and I've invested heavily in maximizing her potential.

The flowing animations of her ultimate attack and the satisfying sound design of her sword strikes make every combat encounter enjoyable. While she requires some skill to master, the payoff in damage and style is absolutely worth it.
